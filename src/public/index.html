Getting here

Event Location

Flying in

Newark Airport (EWR) is the closest to fly into. From the airport, you can take a train (~$20) to Princeton Junction, and from there there are free shuttles to the Westin. You can also take an Uber/Lyft (~$50), or a taxi (~$70). Every option takes about 1 hour without traffic.

Driving

You can get here with your favorite navigation/GPS. There is plenty of free parking at the event location

From NYC or Philly

You can take an NJ transit train from NY Penn Station directly to Princeton Junction on the North East Corridor line. From Philadelphia you can take a SEPTA train from 30th station to Trenton, and from there take a North East Corridor train to Princeton Junction.From Princeton Junction, there are free shuttles to the Westin.

And of course, while in Princeton, why not see Princeton? Princeton is a beautiful college town, with a quaint center, good food, bars, and of course, Princeton University!
